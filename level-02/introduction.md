# Welcome to Vibe Code Academy — Level 2

Level 1 taught you how to build. Level 2 teaches you how to ship something real.

If you completed Level 1, you already know how to talk to Claude Code, write prompts that produce working code, and move from idea to running app faster than most developers. That foundation matters. But there is a gap between "I can build things" and "I have built something a business would pay for." Level 2 closes that gap.

Over the next 20 days you will build **ClearCRM** — a full-featured customer relationship management application. Not a toy. Not a tutorial clone. A real product with contacts, companies, a deals pipeline, an activity log, a live dashboard, email templates, reporting, and CSV import/export. When you are done, it will be running on a live Vercel URL that you can hand to someone and say: "I built this."

Here is what the build stack looks like: Next.js 14 with the App Router, Supabase for your database and authentication, Tailwind CSS for styling, and Vercel for deployment. You will also get hands-on with a new tool this level — **Bolt.new** — an AI-powered browser IDE that lets you scaffold a working prototype in minutes. Week 1 is dedicated entirely to learning Bolt.new so it feels natural before the real build begins. Week 2 through 4 are the CRM build itself.

You will also spend Day 1 installing and activating 10 Claude Code plugins — your plugin power stack. These are tools that give Claude Code superpowers: better code review, smarter testing, faster iteration. Once they are set up, they stay active for every session going forward.

By the time you finish Week 2, you will have a working app with real authentication and a database. By the end of Week 3, the CRM will have its core features. Week 4 is about hardening, testing, and shipping — including a Playwright test pass and a full pre-deploy checklist.

Here is the honest motivating truth: the kind of CRM you will build in 20 days is the kind of product that agencies charge R20,000 to R80,000 to build for clients, or $3,000 to $15,000 in dollar markets. Businesses need CRMs. Small teams, sales consultants, real estate agents, recruitment firms — they all need exactly what you are building. When you finish Level 2, you will have a live portfolio piece that demonstrates you can build it, plus the skills to build it again faster and better.

Show up for the 20 days. Build the project. Deploy it. Then send the link to someone who needs it.

That is how vibe coding turns into real money.
